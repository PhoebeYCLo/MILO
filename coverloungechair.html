Thomas Bentzen

Cover Lounge Chair

$1340

By combining the ideals of Scandinavian craftsmanship with a modern design language, the Cover Lounge Chair has a simple yet refined expression. The design features a grand seat while taking up little space in the room, complemented by its unique details of curved back, wooden armrest covers and slightly curved seat for any private or professional space.

ADD TO CART

The Cover Lounge Chair is made from form pressed wood. The wood is folded over the chair's legs and back rest. This technically innovative wood layering helps lock the elements of the chair together. Upholstered seats are with textiles from Kvadrat or Refine Leather. Oak version: PU lacquered, form pressed oak wood veneer shell. Frame is made from solid beech wood. Colored version: seat and shell is made from ash, while frame and legs are made from solid beech wood.

Total Height: 70.7 cm / 27.8 in
Seat Height: 38.1 cm / 15 in
