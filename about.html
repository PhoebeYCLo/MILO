Our Story

MILO. is rooted in the Scandinavian design tradition characterized by enduring aesthetics, functionality, craftsmanship and an honest expression. By expanding this heritage with forward-looking materials, techniques and bold creative thinking, our ambition is to deliver new perspectives on Scandinavian design.

Our Products

With modern materials, innovative techniques and unique designs, we offers a new perspective on classic Scandinavian home design. Whether you're looking for popular lighting, to furniture, Stacked shelves, Dot hangers and more. MILO. offers Scandi inspiration for your house but a relaxed design that creates a home.
